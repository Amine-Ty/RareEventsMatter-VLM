The peacock spot or olive leaf spot (Spilocaea oleagina) is the most
important olive fungal disease and, together with olive knot, is among
the conditions that most affect the well-being of the olive grove. This
fungal disease is caused by the fungus Spilocaea oleagina. The symptoms
are extremely characteristic and easily recognizable. On olive leaves,
the fungus appears as circular grayish spots, darker in the center and
surrounded by a yellow halo.
